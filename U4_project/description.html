ABOUT THE DESIGNER

Sundry clothing fully captures the sunny, fun-loving spirit of southern California. Born and raised in Aix-au-Provence, Sundry owner and creative director Matthieu Leblan mixes his appreciation for wanderlust and cultural fanfare into his apparel designs. Every piece of Sundry clothing is versatile and relaxed, ready for wear on the beach or for a summer night out.
